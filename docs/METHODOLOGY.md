# Methodology

## Governance model

The framework combines board oversight, operational ownership and a monitoring/intelligence layer. A common taxonomy and central register allow risk information to aggregate across business units.

## Scoring

`inherent risk = likelihood (1-5) × impact (1-5)`

- Green: 1-6 - accept or monitor
- Amber: 7-14 - review and manage
- Red: 15-25 - escalate immediately

Residual risk is the post-control assessment. The example values are not calculated mechanically from a control percentage because control effectiveness requires evidence and judgement.

## FMEA

`RPN = severity × occurrence × detectability`

FMEA is used for process failure modes; bow-tie analysis is more appropriate where causes, a central event, preventive controls, mitigations and consequences must be visible. Scenario and Monte Carlo methods are deferred until the organisation has sufficient historical data.
