# ERM adoption roadmap

| Stage | Management objective | Exit evidence |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Foundation | Approve policy, appetite, taxonomy and ownership | Board approval and owners for every unit |
| Register | Run workshops, score risks and assign treatments | No risk without an owner; red/amber actions agreed |
| Monitoring | Define KRIs and consolidated reporting | Monthly KRIs for red risks; first board review completed |
| Integration | Embed risk criteria in investment, contracts and planning | Risk evidence visible in material decisions |

The academic recommendation targets repeatable/managed maturity within 24 months. Timing must be resourced and validated by the organisation; stage gates matter more than a nominal calendar.
